Dear Sir,
We hereby submit this Notice of Claim to formally record the impact resulting from the issuance
of revised structural drawing packages and the subsequent instructions received.
We would like to clarify that we proceeded with construction works based on the latest approved
structural package received on 27/10/2025, upon which shop drawings were prepared,
submitted, and duly approved. Accordingly, a substantial portion of the column works was
executed at site in compliance with these approved documents.
On 24/03/2026, we received an updated structural package. Upon detailed review, multiple
discrepancies were identified, particularly concerning column dimensions and grid alignments.
Consequently, we submitted a Request for Information (RFl) addressing these conflicts.
in response, we were instructed to disregard the column dimensions indicated in the updated
package and to proceed with column execution as per the previously approved package under
which works had already commenced and progressed.
However, during subsequent discussions with the Consultant regarding additional discrepancies
identified in slab elements, we were instructed to follow the latest updated structural package.
This directive effectively required the application of two different structural packages
simultaneously, which is technically inconsistent and not acceptable from an engineering and
execution standpoint due to the inherent conflicts between both packages.
Furthermore, we would like to highlight that site activities were directly impacted. Shuttering
developed in line with the structural package issued on 27/10/2025.
However, due to the discrepancies arising from the subsequently issued updated package and the
conflicting instructions received, these activities were suspended, as proceeding based on the
earlier approved shop drawings would have resulted in technical inconsistencies.
Accordingly, works remained on hold pending technical clarification and formal direction from
the Consultant, which was only concluded upon the issuance of the final coordinated revised
structural package on 30/04/2026.
