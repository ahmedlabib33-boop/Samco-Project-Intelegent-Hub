With reference to your letter regarding the reinforcement steel supply status and the alleged impact on the
project completion time, kindly note the following:
While it is acknowledged that the second reinforcement steel batch (Ref. 1 above) has eventually been
delivered, the full required quantity was not delivered on the planned date of 26 December 2025 as
in mid-February 2026 (Refs. 2, 3, 4, and 5 above), while the remaining quantities were only delivered on

Accordingly, the delayed supply of reinforcement steel by the Client has materially impacted the progress
of the Works and caused actual delays to project activities. Nevertheless, the Contractor exerted maximum
efforts to mitigate such delays to the extent possible, including the temporary provision of reinforcement
steel during the delayed periods in order to maintain progress of the Works whenever feasible.
However, despite these mitigation efforts, the delay impact could not be fully avoided, as the delayed
supply of reinforcement steel remains a matter beyond the Contractor's control and responsibility.
Meanwhile, the Contractor is currently proceeding with the Works at the maximum possible pace in order
to recover and minimize the delay impacts as far as practicable.
Regarding the third reinforcement steel batch, kindly note that the significant delay in supplying the second
batch, in addition to the Contractor's temporary provision of certain reinforcement steel quantities in order
to maintain the progress of the Works, has affected the quantities and diameters required within the third
batch compared to what had originally been planned.
