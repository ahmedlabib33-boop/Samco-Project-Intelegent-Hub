Dear Sir,
We acknowledge receipt of your above-referenced letter and thank you for your follow-up.
We have taken due note of your observations regarding the non-submission of weekly progress reports for
the period from 12 February 2026 to date.
Please be advised that during the referenced period, the Project has been undergoing intensive and ongoing
coordination discussions related to the overall project phasing, sequencing of works, and the interface
between the civil works and other packages not yet awarded. These circumstances have directly impacted
the preparation of weekly progress reports that accurately reflect the actual progress against the approved
baseline programme, as such reports would have required continuous updates to remain aligned with the
evolving project framework.
Notwithstanding the above, we would like to highlight that despite the challenges affecting the regular
issuance of weekly reports, the Contractor has continued to submit monthly progress reports during the same
ongoing monitoring and documentation.
Nevertheless, and without prejudice to our contractual rights and obligations, we are currently in the process
of preparing and consolidating all outstanding weekly reports in a structured format in line with the
Engineer's requirements under Sub-Clause 4.21 and the General Requirements.
We hereby confirm that all outstanding weekly reports shall be submitted in batches within a period not
exceeding one (1) week from the date of this letter. Furthermore, all future weekly reports will be submitted
regularly and in full compliance with the Contract requirements.
We also propose holding a coordination meeting to agree on a unified reporting format and align
expectations, in order to avoid any future ambiguity.
We trust the above clarifies our position and reflects our commitment to contractual compliance. We also
express our appreciation for the Engineer's role and our keenness to maintain a cooperative working
relationship.
Yours faithfully,
